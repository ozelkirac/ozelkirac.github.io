ABOUT US

OMVISION's History

Welcome to OMVISION! It is all started with a graduation project that is about using deep learning for hemorrhage detection. While working on the graduation project, we applied to TUBITAK's BIGG program and were shortlisted among all the teams. After graduating from university, we founded OMVISION and we are currently working on productization of our graduation design project.

Our main product is a deep learning pipeline for multilabel classification of hemorrhage types. OMVISION is currently working on productization of this deep learning solution.

“It is difficult to think of a major industry that AI will not transform. This includes healthcare, education, transportation, retail, communications, and agriculture. There are surprisingly clear paths for AI to make a big difference in all of these industries.” ― Andrew Ng

OMVISION is also working on the medical segmentation of hemorrhages to aid medical specialists to locate the hemorrhage. Our solution will eliminate the false diagnoses that are caused by simply human nature.

Company aims to create an application for hemorrhage classification and segmentation to instantly diagnose the hemorrhages. This application will be used for a double check mechanism and in some cases as a primary source when needed.
